🛜OXIO: My Experience & Answers to Common Questions👋

I use OXIO for home internet, and it's been great! They offer high-speed plans (up to 1 Gbps) with no contracts, no price hikes, and no hidden fees. They even provide a FREE eero 6 router and straightforward!
The biggest reason? No BS😂 What they advertise is exactly what you get lol no sneaky price increases after a few months, no weird extra charges, and no contracts tying you down. So if you don’t like it, you can just cancel!

🙋Signing Up & Getting Started

Super easy! I just went to their website, put in my address, and signed up online. It only took a few minutes. If you ever need help, their customer service will actually email you back lol, so it’s responsive for me!
It depends on your place! If your home is already set up, you might just need to plug in the modem and go. Either way, they make it pretty hassle free.
Once everything is set up, they send an email letting you know when it's good to go. If you needed a tech, they mention that it usually takes 1-3 hours after they leave for activation, but mine just worked fine right after the activation. You may need a coaxial cable, get it from Temu! It's cheap there!
